## Questions I'd Ask on a Discovery Call

### Technical Capability
1. Walk me through a recent Class II medical device project. What was the device, what did you do, what was the outcome?
2. How do you handle IEC 62304 software documentation? Do you produce the SRS, SDS, architecture docs?
3. What's your design review process? Do you do formal reviews per 21 CFR 820?
4. Your lab does EMC pre-compliance â€” what about electrical safety testing (IEC 60601-1)?

### Quality & Compliance
5. Do you have a quality management system? ISO 13485 certified?
6. How do you handle design history files? Do we own the DHF or do you maintain it?
7. What's your configuration management approach for medical device projects?

### Business & Logistics
8. What's your current capacity? Can you dedicate 2-3 FTE to a 12-month project?
9. What happens if a key engineer leaves mid-project?
10. Do you carry E&O insurance? What's your coverage?
11. How do you handle NDAs and conflict of interest? (Will you work for my competitor?)

### Project Specifics
12. What would a Class II device development cost? Ballpark?
13. What's your typical timeline for a 510(k)-ready design?
14. Do you help with regulatory submission, or just engineering?
